Welcome aboard the NASA Satellite TERRA.

I have been orbiting Earth since 1999, silently watching over our planet.

Your mission: explore my history, meet my instruments, and discover how I protect humanity.

THE CRISIS

In the 1990s, climate change was accelerating. Forests were burning. Glaciers were melting. Pollution was rising.

But Earth had no true global eye. Scientists needed a guardian in space.

SCIENTIFIC INSTRUMENTS

👁️

MODIS

The Eyes

Observes forests, oceans, clouds, ice.

Impact: Tracks deforestation and wildfire spread to protect communities.

🌋

ASTER

The Geologist

Measures ground temperature and elevation.

Impact: Monitors active volcanoes and helps evacuate populations safely.

☁️

MISR

The Cloud Analyst

Detects cloud height and movement.

Impact: Improves storm tracking and weather prediction.

☀️

CERES

The Solar Accountant

Measures how much sunlight Earth absorbs vs reflects.

Impact: Helps scientists understand heatwaves and global warming.

🏭

MOPITT

The Pollution Hunter

Detects carbon monoxide and toxic gases.

Impact: Reveals city pollution levels — Governments use it to enforce cleaner air policies.
